Services

Custom Embedded Systems

Design and development of microcontroller-based devices with ESP32, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi. From single-board prototypes to fully integrated IoT solutions, I handle coding, wiring, and assembly with precision and efficiency.

3D Printing & CAD

Rapid prototyping using PETG, TPU, and FreeCAD. I create enclosures, mounts, functional parts, and detailed mechanical models. Your idea gets translated into a high-quality 3D printable design with an estimate for material and print time.

Automation & Networking

Local network automation, WireGuard device-to-device tunnels, IoT integration, and microcontroller control systems. I implement smart solutions for home, office, or custom project setups, including sensors, relays, and remote monitoring.

Technical Support & Consulting

Assistance with electronics, firmware troubleshooting, prototyping guidance, and project workflow advice. I help you troubleshoot, optimize, and scale your hardware or embedded projects with clear, actionable guidance.
