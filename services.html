Services

Custom embedded systems, electronics, and fabrication work

I offer technical services across embedded systems development, electronics prototyping, 3D printing, and automation. Each project is approached systematically—validating requirements, prototyping core functionality, and delivering working solutions that meet real-world operating conditions.

Embedded Systems Build

Complete development of microcontroller-based devices from concept to working prototype. This includes circuit design, firmware development, component integration, and assembly of functional embedded systems using ESP32, Arduino, or Raspberry Pi platforms.

Typical Projects

  • IoT devices with Wi-Fi or Bluetooth connectivity
  • Sensor monitoring systems with data logging
  • Automation controllers for motors, relays, and actuators
  • Custom alarm or notification systems
  • Environmental monitoring stations

Scope & Considerations

Project complexity is determined by the number of microcontrollers, wireless communication requirements (WiFi, BLE), integration of displays or user interfaces, and power system design (USB, battery, or hybrid). Additional factors include sensor complexity, real-time processing requirements, and whether the system needs remote access or web-based control interfaces.

Coding / Firmware Development

Custom firmware and software development for embedded systems and automation tasks. This includes writing optimized C/C++ code for microcontrollers, Python scripts for automation, and web-based interfaces for device control and monitoring.

Typical Projects

  • Device firmware for ESP32, Arduino, or Raspberry Pi
  • Automation scripts for repetitive tasks or workflows
  • Web dashboards for real-time device monitoring
  • Sensor data processing and analytics
  • Custom control logic for hardware integration

Scope & Considerations

Scope depends on project type (simple automation script vs. multi-page web application), functionality requirements (basic logic vs. complex state management with API integration), and target platform (microcontroller firmware vs. browser-based applications). Projects involving device communication, data storage, or user input validation require additional development time.

Electronics Assembly / Wiring

Circuit assembly, soldering, and wiring for electronics projects. This includes building circuits from schematics, component soldering (through-hole and SMD), breadboard-to-PCB transitions, and enclosure wiring with proper cable management.

Typical Projects

  • Prototype circuit assembly from breadboard designs
  • PCB population and component soldering
  • Device repair and component replacement
  • Custom cable assemblies and wire harnesses
  • Enclosure wiring with strain relief and routing

Scope & Considerations

Complexity is influenced by component count, assembly type (through-hole vs. surface-mount), and whether enclosure integration is required. Projects with many small components, mixed assembly types, or custom enclosure wiring require more time and precision work.

Product Repair

Diagnosis and repair of electronic devices and embedded systems. This includes troubleshooting hardware failures, component replacement, firmware issues, and restoring functionality to damaged or non-working devices.

Typical Projects

  • Consumer electronics repair (controllers, displays, power supplies)
  • Embedded device troubleshooting and component replacement
  • Industrial equipment diagnosis and repair
  • Circuit board trace repair and component reflow
  • Firmware recovery and re-flashing

Scope & Considerations

Repair scope depends on device type (consumer vs. industrial), issue complexity (electrical vs. mechanical vs. combined), and whether diagnostic work is needed to identify the failure. Some repairs may require sourcing replacement components or custom fabrication of unavailable parts.

CAD Modeling / Design

Precision CAD modeling for functional parts, enclosures, and mechanical assemblies. Using FreeCAD and Tinkercad, I create designs optimized for 3D printing with proper tolerances, mounting features, and integration points for electronics.

Typical Projects

  • Custom enclosures for electronics projects
  • Mounting brackets and hardware adapters
  • Mechanical assemblies with interlocking parts
  • Replacement parts for damaged or discontinued components
  • Print-ready STL files with optimized geometry

Scope & Considerations

Project scope is determined by whether you need a new design or modification of existing files, part complexity (simple flat parts vs. complex mechanical assemblies), and whether print-ready export with support structures is required. Designs that integrate with specific hardware or require precise tolerances need additional iteration.

3D Printing

FDM 3D printing services using PETG, PLA, ABS, and TPU materials. I handle file preparation, print optimization, material selection, and deliver finished parts with quality surface finish and dimensional accuracy.

Typical Projects

  • Functional enclosures and protective cases
  • Mechanical parts and replacement components
  • Mounting hardware and brackets
  • Prototype parts for fit testing
  • Custom organizers and fixtures

Scope & Considerations

Cost is calculated based on part dimensions (length, width, height), material choice, and infill requirements. Larger parts, specialized materials like TPU, or high-infill structural components require more material and print time. Print-ready files are preferred, but I can also prepare STL files for optimal printing orientation and support placement.
