Services

Explore the types of projects I can help you with — from custom electronics to rapid prototyping and automation. Click a service to start your project quote.

Custom Embedded Systems

Full design and development of microcontroller-based devices using ESP32, Arduino, and Raspberry Pi. I handle everything from circuit design and firmware to testing, ensuring your custom electronics work exactly as you envision.

3D Printing & CAD

Rapid prototyping with PETG, PLA, and other materials. I design functional enclosures, mounts, and parts using FreeCAD and create optimized 3D prints that combine precision and durability for your projects or products.

Automation & Networking

Development of local network automation, IoT integration, and microcontroller-based control systems. From device-to-device communication with WireGuard to custom automation scripts, I make your systems smarter and easier to manage.

Technical Support & Consulting

Guidance for electronics troubleshooting, firmware issues, prototyping workflows, and project management. I provide detailed advice and hands-on support to ensure your project runs smoothly from concept to completion.
