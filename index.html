About Me

I’m an embedded systems and 3D fabrication enthusiast who loves building physical, functional tech. I work with microcontrollers like the ESP32, Raspberry Pi Zero 2 W, and Arduino-based boards, and I enjoy mixing electronics, coding, 3D design, and fabrication into complete working projects.

My workflow often starts with raw ideas that turn into custom hardware: writing firmware, designing circuits, printing parts, testing components, and refining builds until they’re clean, stable, and useful. Whether it’s motor control, wireless communication, automation, PCB creation, or enclosure design — I like making all the systems work together.

I specialize in practical, hands-on engineering: small embedded devices, microcontroller prototyping, 3D printed housings, and integrating software with hardware for reliable real-world use.
